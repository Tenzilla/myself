About Me

For as long as I can remember, I've always been curious as to how our phones and computers do what we want them to do and how colors and flashy displays were made as if it was painted onto the web page. This steered my down the path to learn more about computers and the different softwares that we all see and use on a daily basis. I graduated in the summer of 2022 with a B.S in Computer Science, I spent a lot of my time over the past 6-7 years doing front end and sometimes back end. Collaborating with others to create amazing projects is what I enjoy the most as I see that it's the best way to learn from each other and share knowledge. My professional experience includes full stack development with a focus on frontend. I also have experience in data automation, and a little bit of UI/UX design. I'm passionate about creating programs that demonstrate correctness, readability and efficiency as well as making it reach the right audiences. In my spare time you can find me in a coffeeshop focused on my laptop trying to debug a piece of code. Outside of coding, snowboarding and hiking are my two favorite hobbies!
